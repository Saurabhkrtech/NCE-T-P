Message from the TPO

Dear Students and Recruiters,

Welcome to the Training and Placement Cell of Nalanda College of Engineering.

As the Training and Placement Officer, I am delighted to extend my warmest greetings to all of you.

At NCE Chandi, we are committed to nurturing and developing the skills and talents of our students, equipping them with the knowledge and experience necessary to excel in their professional careers.

Our institution prides itself on its robust academic programs, state-of-the-art facilities, and a vibrant campus environment that fosters innovation and leadership.

The Training and Placement Cell plays a pivotal role in shaping the career paths of our students. We provide comprehensive support and guidance, from career counseling to soft skills development, ensuring

our students are well-prepared to meet the challenges of the modern workplace. Our collaborations with leading companies and organizations enable us to offer a diverse

range of placement opportunities, tailored to the varied interests and aspirations of our students.

For our esteemed recruiters, we offer a pool of highly talented and motivated individuals who are ready to contribute to your organization's success. Our students undergo rigorous training and possess a blend

of technical proficiency, problem-solving capabilities, and strong ethical values. We invite you to visit our campus and engage with our students, confident that you

will find candidates who are well-suited to your needs.

To our students, I encourage you to take full advantage of the resources and opportunities available through the Training and Placement Cell. Participate actively in the various training programs, workshops,

and recruitment drives organized by the cell. Your dedication and hard work, combined with the support of the cell, will pave the way for a successful and fulfilling career.

In closing, I would like to express my gratitude to all our recruiters for their continued support and to our students for their unwavering commitment to excellence. Together, we can achieve great success and build a brighter future.

Warm regards,

Prof. Nikhil Nigam

Training and Placement Officer

NCE Chandi.
