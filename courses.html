Bachelor of Technology - Computer Science

Explore the world of computer science with our comprehensive B.Tech program. You will learn fundamental concepts such as algorithms, data structures, programming languages, and software engineering practices. The program emphasizes practical learning through projects and internships, ensuring graduates are well-prepared for careers in software development, cybersecurity, data analytics, and more.

Bachelor of Technology - Mechanical Engineering

Our B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering program focuses on the design, analysis, manufacturing, and maintenance of mechanical systems. Students gain hands-on experience with modern tools and technologies, enabling them to innovate in fields such as automotive engineering, robotics, renewable energy, and industrial automation. The curriculum emphasizes both theoretical knowledge and practical skills, preparing graduates for diverse career opportunities.

Bachelor of Technology - Civil Engineering

Study the art and science of designing and constructing infrastructure that is essential for modern society. Our B.Tech in Civil Engineering covers structural engineering, transportation systems, environmental engineering, and construction management. Students engage in hands-on projects and fieldwork, gaining practical skills and knowledge necessary for careers in construction firms, government agencies, consulting firms, and more.

Bachelor of Technology - Electrical & Electronics Engineering

Our B.Tech program in Electrical & Electronics Engineering equips students with a deep understanding of electrical circuits, power systems, electronics, and control systems. The curriculum emphasizes theoretical foundations and practical applications, preparing graduates for careers in power generation and distribution, telecommunications, automation, and renewable energy sectors.

Bachelor of Technology - Computer Science (AI & ML Specialization)

Specialize in artificial intelligence and machine learning within our B.Tech program in Computer Science. Students delve into advanced topics such as neural networks, deep learning, natural language processing, and computer vision. Practical projects and collaborations with industry partners ensure graduates are equipped to tackle real-world challenges in fields such as healthcare, finance, robotics, and smart technologies.

Master of Technology - Electrical & Electronics Engineering

Our M.Tech program in Electrical & Electronics Engineering offers advanced studies in electrical systems, renewable energy sources, power electronics, and control systems. Students engage in research projects and collaborate with industry leaders to address current and future challenges in energy efficiency, sustainable technologies, and smart grid systems. Graduates are prepared for leadership roles in research institutions, multinational corporations, and governmental agencies.

Bachelor of Technology - Aeronautical Engineering

Our B.Tech program in Aeronautical Engineering covers the design, development, testing, and production of aircraft and related systems. Students will gain a strong foundation in aerodynamics, materials science, propulsion, avionics, and flight mechanics. The program emphasizes practical learning through lab work and projects, preparing graduates for careers in the aerospace industry, defense sector, and research organizations.
